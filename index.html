Welcome To Irish Mountain Trail Runners

We’re a welcoming, slightly eccentric (and really good looking) gang of trail and mountain runners based in Dublin. From complete beginners to experienced ultra runners, we celebrate every type of runner who loves getting off the tarmac and into the beautiful Dublin Mountains starting from Cruagh.

Our club runs happen most Wednesdays (evening trails) and Saturday mornings (with options from 4 km up to 17 km).

We also organise regular social events, races and mountain adventures throughout the year.

The atmosphere is supportive, the scenery is stunning, and the banter is top class. So if you're looking for a fun-run, getting fit or some coaching for your next race, come join us and we'll gladly oblige.
