Welcome To Irish Mountain Trail Runners

We're a friendly (really good looking) mountain trail running club with athletes from social to incredibly competitive. We meet on (most) Wednesday and Saturdays as a club with regular club events inbetween. All are welcome, but please confirm with Lar prior to arrival.
