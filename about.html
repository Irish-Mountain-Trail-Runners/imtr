About Our Club

Irish Mountain Trail Runners was founded to bring together people who share a passion for trail and mountain running. We regularly meet twice a week on Wednesdays and Saturdays with our trail runs focusing on Cruagh Park. All the runs are guided with a great spirit of camaraderie between all the runners.
