Frequently Asked Questions

1. Do I need to be an experienced trail runner to join?

Not at all! We welcome runners of all abilities, from complete beginners to experienced ultra runners. Our Wednesday and Saturday runs include different pace groups so everyone can enjoy the trails.

2. Where and when do you meet?

We meet at Cruagh Woods every Wednesday at 7:00 PM and every Saturday morning. Saturday offers multiple distance options, including beginner, intermediate and experienced groups.

3. Do I have to become a member before attending a run?

No. New runners are always welcome to come along and see what the club is all about before deciding whether they'd like to become a member.

4. What should I bring to my first trail run?

Wear comfortable running clothes and trail running shoes if you have them. Bring water, dress appropriately for the weather, and most importantly, bring a positive attitude—we'll take care of the rest!
