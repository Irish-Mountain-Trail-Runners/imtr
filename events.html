Our Weekly Sessions

Wednesday Evening Trail Session

Every Wednesday we do our weekly 6km guided trail session from Cruagh car park. Run starts at 7, get there a bit earlier to warm up.

This is subject to change, please let us know in advance if you're going to join us.

Saturday Morning Session

On Saturdays we meet at 08:30 at Cruagh for a 4km and a 7km guided run and at 09:30 for a 10km(ish) run. For the braver and more athletic ones, come join us for both.

This is subject to change, please let us know in advance if you're going to join us.
